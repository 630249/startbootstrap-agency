    Bed Bugs

    All you need to know: health concerns, solutions and where to find them.

    Bed bugs are parasites and are so small they often can not be seen by the naked eye; it's extremely difficult for a non-professional to get rid of them.

    • The bed bug lifecycle lasts almost 1 month, and leave many signs. They affect your sleeping habits, and cause irritation/ itching. They may also affect your hygiene to some extent.
    • You can tell if your bitten by (1) small bites that become inflaned and itchy (something like mosquito bites). (2) These bites may then transfer to bigger, redder lumps. Skin problems may occur, and so it is dire to get rid of them, and have a medical proffesional such as a doctor look at your health concerns.

    Rodents

    All you need to know: health concerns, solutions and where to find them.

    Rats are creatures that spread so quickly and once they do, they usually need an experienced professional to properly treat them. They enter homes through cracks and holes found in walls, floors and foundations. Homeowners commonly do not recognize mouse holes until other signs of infestation appear. Due to the flexible body of a rat, it can fit just about anywhere and it tends to have many babies. Rodents are different than mice, please do note.

    • Rats can pose as a dangerous health concern as they can carry disease and sicknesses, in their fur, from one place to another. Ensure uses the most effective and eco-friendly treatment method for rats, at a very economical rate.

    Wasps

    All you need to know: health concerns, solutions and where to find them.

    Wasps are a type of insect that whether outdoors or indoors, can physically harm or hurt you. Being at times aggressive towards humans, wasps tend to live in colonies within hives. Wasp infestations are best handled by your pest management professional, someone who has the experience and equipment to safely, effectively and efficiently resolve your concerns. Ensure uses a very helpful procedure in resolving your problem, and makes sure you see your results.

    Cockroaches

    All you need to know: health concerns, solutions and where to find them.

    Cockroaches are one of the most invasive species that can invade anywhere, anytime. Once they start to spread, they are extremely difficult to control and most probably will NOT go away till you call a professional. Ensure uses the most effective method in treating cockroaches and helps solve your problem.

    • If you see any insect droppings, insect eggs, or smell any unusual odor, (around your kitchen and basement) you most probably have been invaded by cockroaches. The most common types of cockroach are the German, Oriental, and American Cockroach in Southern Ontario. You can minimally help this common problem by covering food or denying cockroaches easy access to shelter.

    Fleas

    All you need to know: health concerns, solutions and where to find them.

    There are 2,500 species of these small flightless insects that survive. Fleas survive by sucking blood and adult fleas grow to about 3 mm or .12 in length. They are usually brown, and have bodies that are "flattened" sideways, or narrow, enabling them to move through their host's fur or feathers. They lack wings, but they are able to leap a distance of some 50 times their body length.

    • Fleas' larvae are worm-like with no limbs; they have chewing mouthparts and feed on organic debris left on their host's skin. Please contact us for more information regarding fleas, where exactly they are and what the process of treatment is like.
